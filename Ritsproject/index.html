WhatWe Do

Manufacturing Execution System

Core Aspect of Manufacturing is Data driven productivity and efficiency. Success of the MES implementation relies on Integrated Solution across Shop floor to Top Floor. RitsConsulting is one of the world leading Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) / Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) specialists with global delivery capabilities.We have a dedicated and expertised Manufacturing Execution System (MES) competency working on manufacturing solutions, worldwide. Our MES competency delivers MES solutions to customers in multiple industry domains such as, Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Life Sciences, Automotive, Aerospace, Semiconductor and HiTech.

Industry 4.0

IoT promises digital transformation of manufacturing operations through deployment of edge computing, sensors, and cloud-based solutions. Fragmented, heterogeneous manufacturing IT landscapes are standardized to simplify use and maintenance. The extended enterprise is seamlessly and ubiquitously visible, resulting in new business opportunities previously unimagined. RITS consulting team works closely with SAP in implementation of Industrial IoT through SAP Manufacturing under SAP Leonardo platform.

Plant Automation

Automation is the art of transforming manually performed business activities into processes that are orchestrated and controlled through software solutions. Both simple and complex scenarios are standardized. Scenarios are automatically executed by using integrated components and applications, such as MES, workflow and rules engines, and real-time event processing.
